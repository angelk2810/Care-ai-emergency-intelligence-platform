# 🚑 CARE-AI  
### Intelligent Emergency Triage & Risk Assessment Platform

AI-powered emergency intelligence for **faster, smarter, life-saving decisions.**

---

## 🧠 Overview

**CARE-AI** is a multi-page **AI-powered Emergency Triage Intelligence Platform** designed to assist in **real-time medical urgency classification**.

The system analyzes patient symptoms and provides instant medical risk insights to support faster triage decisions during emergency situations.

### It performs:

- 🩺 Real-time **Symptom Analysis**
- 🚨 **Urgency Level Classification** *(Low / Moderate / Critical)*
- 📊 **Risk Confidence Scoring**
- 📝 **Structured Medical Report Generation**
- 🧾 **AI-assisted Clinical Summary**

CARE-AI helps reduce delays in emergency response and assists healthcare professionals with **AI-backed medical intelligence within seconds.**

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Emergency rooms often face:

- Patient overload
- Delayed triage decisions
- Inconsistent urgency classification
- Lack of structured early-stage reports

⚠️ Even small delays in triage can cost lives.

**CARE-AI assists medical professionals by providing AI-powered urgency prediction and structured medical intelligence instantly.**
